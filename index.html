About

A heartwarming photo of two Shiba Inus with their heads pressed together went viral on social media, earning 22,800 shares and 92,600 likes! Taken by photographer Tetsuya, this photo captures the unique charm of Japanese Shiba Inus, sparking global interest in preserving this ancient breed. The $SANAN project is dedicated to promoting Shiba Inu culture, supporting adoption, and protecting Shiba Inus, helping more people to discover and care for this unique canine companion.
